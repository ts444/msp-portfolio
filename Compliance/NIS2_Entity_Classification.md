## Overview
NIS2 Entity Classification & Regulatory Advisory provides mid-market organisations with expert guidance to determine their classification status under the NIS2 Directive (Essential vs Important entity), fulfil supervisory authority notification and registration obligations, and scope the full set of Art.21 security measures and Art.23 incident reporting duties that apply to them. The service removes the dependency on external legal counsel for the classification prerequisite, enabling an end-to-end NIS2 compliance pathway under a single MSP engagement.

## What's Included
- Entity classification self-assessment: sector and subsector mapping against Annex I / Annex II thresholds (size criteria, criticality, cross-border impact)
- Essential vs Important entity determination with documented rationale and evidence pack
- Supervisory authority notification and registration support: preparation and submission of required registration filings
- NIS2 obligation scoping review: full analysis of which Art.21 security measures and Art.23 incident reporting timelines apply to the entity
- Regulatory deadline tracking and calendar for compliance milestones
- Gap analysis between current security posture and applicable Art.21 obligations
- Ongoing monitoring for classification changes triggered by corporate events (M&A, headcount growth, new sectors)

## What's Not Included
- Legal representation before supervisory authorities or courts
- DPIA or GDPR compliance work (see [[GDPR_Gap_Assessment]])
- ISO 27001 certification delivery (see [[ISMS_Programme]])
- Incident response or forensic investigation (see [[Incident_Response]])
- Political / lobbying advisory for regulatory policy changes

## Dependencies
- [[IT_Risk_Management]] — existing IT risk register is used as primary input to the Art.21 obligation gap analysis
- [[ISMS_Programme]] — ISMS scope and controls directly satisfy a large portion of Art.21 security measures
- [[Regulatory_Deadline_Tracking]] — classification milestones are fed into the cross-framework deadline tracker
