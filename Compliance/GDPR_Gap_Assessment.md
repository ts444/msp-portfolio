## Overview
GDPR Gap Assessment & Privacy Compliance provides mid-market organisations with a structured assessment of their current data processing practices against GDPR obligations, identifying gaps in data mapping, lawful basis documentation, subject rights handling, and breach notification readiness. The service delivers a prioritised remediation plan and the foundational documentation artefacts required to demonstrate accountability to data protection authorities.

## What's Included
- Data mapping and Records of Processing Activities (RoPA): identification and documentation of all personal data processing activities, data flows, and third-party processors
- Lawful basis review: assessment of the documented basis for each processing activity (consent, contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation) with recommendations for gaps
- Data subject rights readiness: process review and gap remediation for access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection rights
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) support: screening, scope, and facilitated DPIA for high-risk processing activities
- Breach notification readiness: incident classification criteria, 72-hour notification process, DPA and data subject communication templates
- Privacy notices and consent mechanism review
- Processor agreement review checklist (Article 28 compliance)
- Remediation roadmap with risk-ranked action items and effort estimates

## What's Not Included
- Legal representation before data protection authorities
- Drafting of binding legal agreements (requires qualified legal counsel)
- Ongoing DPO-as-a-service (available as a separate retainer)
- US state privacy law (CCPA, CPRA) or non-EU jurisdiction compliance
