## Overview
IT Project Management provides structured planning, execution oversight, and stakeholder communication for IT initiatives that are too complex or cross-functional to be handled as routine service requests. For mid-market organizations that lack a dedicated PMO, the service gives technology projects the governance discipline needed to deliver on time, within scope, and with proper change control — from infrastructure refresh programs to security uplift initiatives and SaaS migrations.

## What's Included
- Project initiation: scope definition, stakeholder identification, RACI matrix, and project charter
- Project planning: milestone scheduling, dependency mapping, resource allocation, and risk register
- Execution oversight: weekly status reporting, action item tracking, and issue escalation management
- Vendor and third-party coordination on behalf of the customer during project delivery
- Budget tracking against approved project spend with variance reporting
- Project closure: deliverable sign-off, lessons learned documentation, and knowledge transfer to operations
- Portfolio-level visibility: active project status dashboard for IT leadership

## What's Not Included
- Programme management for multi-year transformation programs requiring dedicated programme governance
- Business case development and investment justification analysis
- Procurement and contract negotiation with vendors (supported by the MSP, not owned)
- Organizational change management and end-user adoption programs
- Agile coaching or software development sprint management
