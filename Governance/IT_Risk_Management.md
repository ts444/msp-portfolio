## Overview
IT Risk Management provides a structured, ongoing approach to identifying, assessing, treating, and tracking IT-related risks across the managed environment. For mid-market organizations facing growing regulatory scrutiny, cyber insurance requirements, and board-level risk reporting demands, a maintained IT risk register and a consistent risk treatment process turn an ad-hoc, reactive posture into a defensible governance program.

## What's Included
- IT risk register design, maintenance, and quarterly review facilitation
- Risk assessment methodology aligned to ISO 27005 or NIST SP 800-30 (customer's choice)
- Risk identification workshops covering technology, process, and third-party risks
- Risk treatment plan development: accept, mitigate, transfer, or avoid — with owner assignment and target dates
- Residual risk tracking with status updates after treatment actions are completed
- Cyber insurance renewal support: risk register extracts and control effectiveness evidence
- Annual IT risk report for executive and board consumption

## What's Not Included
- Enterprise risk management (ERM) covering non-IT business risks
- Legal or regulatory compliance gap assessments beyond IT controls mapping
- Third-party vendor due diligence assessments and supplier risk scoring
- Actuarial modeling for cyber risk quantification (e.g., FAIR methodology)
- Internal audit services or external certification body assessments
